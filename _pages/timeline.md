## Project Timeline

### Phase 1 — Quantitative Spatial Analysis (Months 1–5)
**Status: In Progress**

Construction of the institutional invisibility gradient. Spatial analysis at three scales (block, UPZ, locality). Production of maps and tables. Drafting Paper 2.

**Key outputs:**
- Final gradient map with layer variables for all households
- Maps at block, UPZ, and locality scale
- Deprivation profile tables by layer

---

### Phase 2 — Qualitative Participatory Fieldwork (Months 5–9)
**Status: Upcoming**

In-depth interviews, collaborative mapping, urban walks, participatory photography with disposable cameras. Transcription and organization of materials.

**Recruitment:** Stratum 3–4 households with at least one informal or underemployed worker, active private education spending and/or mortgage. Poverty language is NOT used in recruitment.

**Sequence:** initial interview → disposable camera → urban walk → mapping workshop → return interview with photos

---

### Phase 3 — Integrated Analysis & Knowledge Transfer (Months 8–12)
**Status: Upcoming**

Thematic coding. Triangulation between components. Analysis of photo + narrative pairs. Drafting Paper 3.

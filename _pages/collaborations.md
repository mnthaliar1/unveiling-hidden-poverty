## Institutional Partners

### [Centro de Estudios Manuel Ramírez (CEMR)](https://www.ce-mr.org)
Lead executing institution. CEMR hosts the project and provides institutional anchoring in Colombia, territorial knowledge, and community research networks.

---

### [Maastricht University](https://www.maastrichtuniversity.nl)
Academic partner. The project is affiliated with the Maastricht Graduate School of Governance / UNU-MERIT, bringing expertise in social protection, poverty measurement, and development policy.

---

### Programa Álvaro Reyes para Jóvenes Investigadores
Funding institution. This program supports emerging researchers at CEMR working on socially relevant questions in the Colombian context.

---

### Secretaría Distrital de Integración Social (SDIS)
Producer of the ILSIS territorial index and executor of social policy in Bogotá. Key institutional interlocutor for the policy translation of research findings.

---

### DANE — Departamento Administrativo Nacional de Estadística
Source of Sisbén IV microdata. The national statistical agency responsible for Colombia's household survey infrastructure.
